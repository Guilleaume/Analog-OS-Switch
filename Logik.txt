ANALOG-OS (v1.2p) - SCHEMATIC LOGIC & PIN ASSIGNMENTS (IEC)
===============================================================================

[SWITCH DEFINITION: DPDT (Push-Pull, Push-Push or Mini-Toggle)]

   11 -> NC (Normally Closed)       21 -> NC (Normally Closed)
   12 -> COM (Common/Center)        22 -> COM (Common/Center)
   14 -> NO (Normally Open)         24 -> NO (Normally Open)

   ON-ON Logic:       Pos A = COM <-> NO  |  Pos B = COM <-> NC
   ON-OFF-ON Logic:   Pos A = COM <-> NO  |  MID = Open  |  Pos B = COM <-> NC

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
VERSION 1.0 (Basic Analog-OS Switch)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Goal: Toggle between 50s and Modern Wiring.

PIN | ASSIGNMENT
----|--------------------------------------------------------------------------
21  | (NC) Wire to Lug 3 of Volume Pot (Input)
22  | (COM) Wire to Lug 2 of Tone Pot
24  | (NO) Wire to Lug 2 of Volume Pot (Output)

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
VERSION 1.1 / 1.2p (Integrated Treble-Bleed + Intelligent Load)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Goal: Modern/50s toggle. Treble-Bleed active ONLY in Modern (Pos A).
Note: v1.2p uses ON-OFF-ON for "Tone-Bypass" in Middle Position.

PIN | ASSIGNMENT
----|--------------------------------------------------------------------------
12  | (COM) Wire to Lug 2 of Volume Pot (Output)
14  | (NO) Treble-Bleed (Side A)
21  | (NC) Wire to Lug 3 of Volume Pot (Input)
22  | (COM) Wire to Lug 2 of Tone Pot AND Treble-Bleed (Side B)
24  | (NO) Wire to Lug 2 of Volume Pot (Output)

* LOAD CORRECTION (v1.2p only): In "OFF" (Mid), Tone Pot is isolated.
  Perfect for series-switched Single-Coils to restore 250k load.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
VERSION 1.2 (Capacitor Cascade)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Goal: Switch 1 = v1.1 Logic | Switch 2 = Frequency Shift

PIN | ASSIGNMENT (Switch 2)
----|--------------------------------------------------------------------------
21  | (NC) Capacitor 1 (Other end to ground)
22  | (COM) Wire to Lug 3 of Tone Pot
24  | (NO) Capacitor 2 (Other end to ground)
